How It Works

Your CGPA is calculated using the formula: Total Points / Total Credits.

  1. Select your CGPA scale by choosing a preset option or selecting "Other" to enter a custom scale.
  2. Enter your current CGPA and completed credits.
  3. Provide GPA, credit details, and optional course names for each course taken this semester.

Example: If your current CGPA is 3.50 with 90 completed credits and you take 3 new courses:

  • Course 1: GPA 4.0, Credit 3
  • Course 2: GPA 3.7, Credit 2
  • Course 3: GPA 3.0, Credit 4

Your updated CGPA will be 3.46.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Will this CGPA match my official transcript?

While this calculator uses standard formulas, official transcripts may vary based on your institution's rounding or weighting policies.

Can I see my CGPA in multiple scales?

Absolutely! Simply select a different scale from the dropdown (or enter a custom scale) and recalculate with your course details.

Is my data stored or shared?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser, and no data is sent to any server.
