How It Works

Your CGPA is calculated using the formula: Total Points / Total Credits.

Steps:

  1. We multiply the GPA of each course by its credit to get the total points for that course.
  2. The sum of all points is divided by the sum of all credits to get your updated CGPA.

Example:

If you have a current CGPA of 3.50 with 90 completed credits and you take 3 new courses with the following GPA and credit:

  • Course 1: GPA 4.0, Credit 3
  • Course 2: GPA 3.7, Credit 2
  • Course 3: GPA 3.0, Credit 4

Your updated CGPA would be: 3.46
