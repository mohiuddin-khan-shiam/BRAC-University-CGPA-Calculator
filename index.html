How It Works

Your CGPA is calculated using the formula: Total Points / Total Credits.

  1. Multiply each course's GPA by its credit to get the total points for that course.
  2. Add all total points and divide by the total credits to get your updated CGPA.

Example: If your current CGPA is 3.50 with 90 completed credits and you take 3 new courses:

  • Course 1: GPA 4.0, Credit 3
  • Course 2: GPA 3.7, Credit 2
  • Course 3: GPA 3.0, Credit 4

Your updated CGPA will be 3.46.
